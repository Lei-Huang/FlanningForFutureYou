My Career Portfolio

Once you have settled into your studies and life at ANU, it is worth taking the time to think about what you want to get out of your education. You may have a clear idea of your career direction or you may not be sure what you want to do after university.
It's never too early to start planning your career.This site will provide the steps recommended to start planning your career and are skills you can use for the rest of your career. Follow the steps on this site and see your progress as you go.

  • Current Profile
    Your major, working experience(casual/full-time), volunteer experience

  • Understanding Yourself
    Including a career value survey and link to book a career planning appointment

  • Career Goals
    Link to some tools about setting up career goals

  • Engaging with Employers
    Social networking, Resources for narrowing down target list of employers

  • Researching Potential Jobs
    Links to workshops in career hub (Resume, graduation program).

  • Getting Ready for Graduate Employment
    Links to interview skill workshops and graduate programs
